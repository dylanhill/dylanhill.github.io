Lesson 2: Strategies

In this lesson, we explore some of the strategies that are available to mitigate the digital threats that exist when conducting sensitive work.

Many of the protection strategies covered in this lesson are interrelated with each other and your greatest protections come from implementing multiple strategies together. In addition, the strategies that you might use to protect yourself from one threat can overlap with the strategies used to protect from another threat.
