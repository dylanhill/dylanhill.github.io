Protecting Yourself in the Digital

Threats

This lesson we will explore some of the most common and significant risks that exist when it is necessary to protect your privacy, files and communications in the digital space.

It should be noted, that many of these risks are interrelated with each other. In addition, the strategies that you might use to protect yourself from one risk can overlap with the strategies used to protect from another risk. For the sake of clarity, we have defined categories of risks and provided examples in this lesson. We will then cover in more detail various protection strategies that can be used to mitigate risks in the following lesson.
